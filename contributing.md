# Contributing to ContextStore

Thank you for your interest in this project.  
To maintain clear and centralized ownership of the codebase, all contributions
must follow the guidelines below.

## Contribution Policy

1. **All contributions are received under the Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO) 1.1.**
   By submitting a pull request, you certify that:
   - You wrote the code yourself, or
   - You have the right to submit it under the open-source license of the project, and
   - You grant the project owner the right to use, distribute, modify, and relicense your contribution.


2. **License.**  
   The project is licensed under the MIT License.  
   By contributing, you agree that your contributions are provided under this license
   and that the project owner retains copyright ownership.

3. **No unsolicited large features.**  
   Open an issue before proposing major changes to ensure alignment.

## Pull Request Requirements

- Keep patches focused and contained.
- Include tests where relevant.
- Ensure code follows project style and passes all checks.
